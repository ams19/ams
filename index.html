LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Students and Librarians can sign in or sign up to the app. Librarians can issue books to students using this app. Email alerts will be send to the students on the last due date of return and after which fine will be charged at 5Rs/day for late return. KEY FEATURES:

  • Accurate fine calculation for late return.
  • Developed using Java, Javafx, SceneBuilder, MySQL etc.

SMART STREET LIGHTING

Smart street lighting refers to public street lighting that adapts to movement by pedestrians, cyclists and cars. Intelligent street lighting, also referred to as adaptive street lighting, dims when no activity is detected, but brightens when movement is detected. This type of lighting is different from traditional, stationary illumination, or dimmable street lighting that dims at pre-determined times because of its dynamic movement dependent behavior. This was my final year project work.

KEY FEATURES:

  • Huge Energy savings up to 30-40%.
  • Excellent central operations and maintenance of street lights.
  • Real time fault detection and Smart communication.
  • Reduced Carbon Emission.
  • Developed using Tinkercard (Simulation platform), Python, Thingspeak (Cloud Service), etc.
